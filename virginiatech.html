Virginia Tech

Activities and clubs I have been involved in.

Computer Science Mentoring Club

Computer Science Mentoring is a club that facilitate mentee and mentorship relationship between upper and lower classmen who are pursuing a Computer Science major or minor. I am currently the Vice President of this organization, the biggest impact I have had in this club is that I facilitated an external mentorship program with our current mentees/mentors. This external mentorship program connected current Virginia Tech students with alumni, the students and alum were met up two times via online chat or video chat.

Volunteer at the Center of Human Computer Interaction at Virginia Tech

During my freshmen and sophmore year I volunteered at the HCI department at Virginia Tech. This involved sitting in on weekly meetings in order to learn and try to assist the current graduate students. Over the school year we competed in the 3DUI competition in China, where we were tasked to create a virtual reality escape room expierence. More information about the project can be located here.

Club Tennis

I play club tennis at Virginia Tech and I am currently the treasurer. Virginia Tech play competitive tennis across the mid atlantic. Each week during the regular season some of our team travels to compete, I have been a part of this team since my freshmen year.
